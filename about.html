About Me

I've been passionately working in game design for eight years now, starting with RPG Maker in 2016.

I have since gotten involved in all aspects of game-making. However, my interest and passion has peaked when in comes to programming.

I am currently working as one of two programming directors at MSU's student-led game development club "Spartasoft Studio". This role involves managing and leading a group of twelve skilled programmers to create a polished game over the course of an academic year at MSU.

I am committed to bridging the gap between programming and design, and am always striving to learn more. I am also a passionate problem solver ready to bring a unique perspective and talent to the game development field.

I have considerable experience with the following programming languages: C#, C++, html, CSS, and Python.
