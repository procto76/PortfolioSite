Trick or Treat Fighters X 3D

Trick or Treat Fighters X 3D is a Halloween themed candy collecting party game, supporting 2-4 players. I worked on this project as a gameplay programmer, implementing mechanics such as: house spawning/looting, candy spawning, cars, player death, chainsaw enemy AI, and more. I am very proud of this project, and highly recommend checking it out! Some of our team (myself included) are continuing development on this project for a full release on Steam, which will include new gamemodes and online play.
