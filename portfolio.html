Portfolio

Here are some game projects I've worked on:


Backyard Brawl

Trick or Treat Fighters X 3D is a Halloween themed candy collecting party game, supporting 2-4 players. I worked on this project as a gameplay programmer, implementing mechanics such as: house spawning/looting, candy spawning, cars, player death, chainsaw enemy AI, and more. I am very proud of this project, and highly recommend checking it out! Some of our team (myself included) are continuing development on this project for a full release on Steam, which will include new gamemodes and online play.

itch.io link






Adventurer's Anomalous

Adventurer's Anomalous is a 2D fantasy guild management game where you take on the role of guild leader. This systems-heavy game has you manage your recruits and income to expand your guild and maintain the health and happiness of your members. I did a large portion of systems and UI programming on this project, using scriptable objects to store and interact with guild data.

itch.io link






Supermarket Sprint

Supermarket Sprint is a top-down puzzle game where the player is given a task to find various items in a supermarket. This game prototype was developed with classmate Andrew Tran in November 2022. I worked on this project as a lead programmer and designer, and created most of the game systems, including the player movement, task system, item spawning, collection, UI, and aisle mechanics.

itch.io link




The Other Side

"The Other Side" is a work in progress turn-based RPG solo project I've been working on over the course of two years. It currently has a playtime of about three hours. This project taught me much of what I know regarding technical design, as that's how I've spent most of my time on it.
